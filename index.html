ABOUT THIS REPOSITORY

This is a living documentation of process — an open-source record of one educator-artist-researcher's exploration of AI-assisted interactive code generation as a medium for expressing ideas about human learning, meaning-making, and information literacy.

These interactive models were generated collaboratively with Claude (Anthropic) and ChatGPT (OpenAI), iterated, and reflected upon as part of an ongoing inquiry into what AI-assisted code generation can and cannot teach. The conceptual framework, prompting, curation, and process reflection are the work of the human author.

The central argument: engineers build M (material) and operate P (process). Power over Process is not the same as power over Meaning. Meaning is the domain of living minds — arts education, contemplative practice, and the cultivation of imagination.

PROCESS NOTE — These explorations are documented openly so others can examine not just the outputs but the process that produced them. A learner encountering these tools without prior experience of coding, drawing, and making will have a different — and likely shallower — encounter than one who brings those subconscious models. That gap is itself a demonstration of the framework.
