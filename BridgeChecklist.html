The Bridge Checklist

A collective ritual for navigating AI-mediated events with clarity, integrity, and care

PANEL 01
⊕
Gyroscope
Stability and co-regulation come first
R
Regulation
"What does my body tell me before I interpret anything?"
absent: flooding · shutdown · shame
C
Resonant Coupling
"Who do I trust here, and have I actually talked to them?"
absent: dominance loops · performative agreement
The first safe pause is not for analysis. It is for stabilizing the organism and the social field.
PANEL 02
◎
Radar
Widen sensing — hold fog — include the shadow field
U
Uncertainty
"What do I genuinely not know yet?"
absent: forced closure · false certainty
S
Signal Coverage
"Where did this come from, and who benefits from me believing it?"
absent: tunnel vision · narrative capture
E
Externalities
"Who is paying a cost that isn't visible in this picture? What is being displaced, onto whom, over what time horizon?"
absent: moral blindness · boundary errors
If you don't include the shadow field, you will mistake local optimization for wisdom.
PANEL 03
✦
Compass
Update models · validate in reality · choose kind action
P
Model Flexibility
"What assumption am I most attached to right now?"
absent: identity defense · rigid certainty
V
Validation
"What's the smallest action I could take to test whether I'm right?"
absent: compliance theater · false authority
K
Kindness Gain
"What would honest and kind look like here — warm truth, firm limits, and a commitment to repair?"
absent: cruelty · sentiment · moral cover
Kind action is the closing move of the learning cycle — not the starting performance.
